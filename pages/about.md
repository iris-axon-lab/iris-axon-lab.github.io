I'm **Iris Shen** — Principal Scientist working on agentic AI systems at scale.

My current work covers the full agentic runtime layer: memory and context management, tool and skill orchestration, planning and reasoning, and long-running task lifecycle. The through-line across all of it is a single question: *how do systems know what they know, and act reliably on it?*

---

### Three careers, one question

**Now — Agentic AI Infrastructure**
Building the runtime layer for M365 Copilot's agentic systems. Tens of millions of users. The hard problems are not the models — they're the memory, the orchestration, and the failure modes nobody planned for.

**Before — Knowledge Graphs at Microsoft Research**
Built billion-scale academic knowledge graphs adopted by the OECD and the Stanford AI Index. Learned that knowledge representation is never just a data problem.

**Before that — Operations Research**
PhD from USC. Learned that the best model is the one that actually gets used.
